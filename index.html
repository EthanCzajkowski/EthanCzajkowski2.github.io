Sword Art Online

Sword Art Online was one of the most anticiapated releases of the season, the 10000 copies sold for its initial release selling like hotcakes, but when the players finally arrived in the first real deal VRMMO a grim development came to be, now trapped inside the virtual world where death is a real consequence, their only way out, is to beat the game

One Punch Man

In a world which killer monsters roaming the streets are a daily occurence, heroes rise up to combat them, this story revolves around one man possesing absolute power, and how he lives his life, slaying monsters thanklessly and struggling to get by, he fights for neither fortune nor fame, he's simply a hero for fun

Fairy Tail

In the land of Fiore, magic is real, wizards of all kinds ply their trade and make a living using magic, in many places these wizards band together as wizard guilds, places for wizards to converge and receive jobs as well as find a place to belong, the greatest of these guilds is known as Fairy Tail, join Fairy Tail in this mystical world on their adventures and learn the joy of friendship and found family
