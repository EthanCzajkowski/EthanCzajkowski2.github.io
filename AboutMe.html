About Me

My Name Is Ethan Joseph Czajkowski

I was born March 5th 2003 and developed a love of anime after I initially began encountering it on Netflix when I was around 10 years old.

My first anime was Sword Art Online and ever since anime has been the main form of media I consumed, eventually I started reading Manga as well and, in my thirst for more content, even Fanfiction.

The reason this site came to be is because of an assignment from my web development class in college, I was told to make my own website, whatever I wanted it to be.

The only thing I could think of was my love for anime, so I decided my site would be a way for me to share what I love with others
